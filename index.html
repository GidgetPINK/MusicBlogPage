What is About the Music?

Music has been a big part of my life. There's a song connected to every special moment in my life - 1st break up...there's a song for that. The day of my High School graduation....I have a song for that. Music truly creates a soundtrack for our lives and as time goes on and memories fade it's those special songs that can pull those lost memories back to the forefront of our minds and put us right back in that moment.


I created About the Music as a place to celebrate and remember those artists and songs that created the soundtrack to those special life moments
